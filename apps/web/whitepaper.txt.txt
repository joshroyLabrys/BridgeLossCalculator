## 15. Risk Factors and Mitigation

### Market Risks

**Risk: Economic Downturn Reducing Property Transaction Volume**
- Mitigation: Variable cost structure, geographic diversification, multiple revenue streams, counter-cyclical opportunities
- Impact: Reduced volume would proportionally reduce costs while maintaining gross margins

**Risk: Competitive Response from Established Platforms**
- Mitigation: Blockchain advantage creates sustainable technical differentiation, network multiplier effects, first-mover advantage
- Impact: Established platforms face revenue model conflicts (agent bias elimination will undermine existing revenue model)

**Risk: Agent Resistance to Transparency**
- Mitigation: Quality agents welcome transparency as competitive advantage, economic incentives align (more leads for better performers), foundation programme demonstrates value
- Impact: Market self-correction and discipline effects compel all agents to comply

### Operational Risks

**Risk: Technology Scaling Challenges**
- Mitigation: Cloud infrastructure (AWS/Azure), experienced technology team, regular penetration testing, disaster recovery planning

**Risk: Call Centre Quality and Capacity**
- Mitigation: Existing 30-month track record with lead verification, comprehensive training programmes, technology-assisted verification and automation, phased scaling aligned with growth

### Regulatory and Compliance Risks

**Risk: Changes to Property Industry Regulation**
- Mitigation: Proactive regulatory engagement, legal counsel specialising in property industry (Sierra Legal), platform designed for transparency (aligned with regulatory trends), flexible architecture
- Impact: Transparency focus aligns with regulatory direction; positioned favourably

**Risk: Privacy and Data Protection Compliance**
- Mitigation: Privacy-by-design architecture from inception, regular privacy audits, clear data usage policies and consent mechanisms, secure data storage with encryption

### Financial Risks

**Risk: Slower Than Projected Revenue Growth**
- Mitigation: Conservative financial with multiple scenarios modelled, monthly milestone tracking, multiple revenue streams, low burn rate and path to profitability

**Risk: Higher Than Expected Customer Acquisition Costs**
- Mitigation: Affiliate network provides scalable variable-cost acquisition, multiple marketing channels, network effects reduce acquisition costs over time, foundation programme provides low-cost initial agent base
- Impact: LTV/CAC ratio >(20:1) provides a substantial buffer for higher costs
